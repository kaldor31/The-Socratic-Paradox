# The Socratic Paradox

A private digital journal for examining thoughts through the Socratic method. It helps you slow down a thought, question it, notice cognitive distortions, and write a calmer, more rational conclusion.

## What you can do

- **Capture a thought** — write down whatever is on your mind.
- **Answer Socratic questions** — the app guides you through classic questions like evidence, alternatives, and consequences.
- **Tag distortions** — mark the thought patterns that show up (e.g., catastrophizing, all-or-nothing thinking).
- **Write a synthesis** — conclude with a balanced perspective.
- **Keep a journal** — revisit, favorite, or delete past sessions.
- **Switch languages** — the app supports English and Russian.

## Privacy & security

- Accounts are email-based with verification codes.
- Passwords are hashed with bcrypt.
- Sessions are tied to your account and protected by authentication.
